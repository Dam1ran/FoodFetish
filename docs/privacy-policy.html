Privacy Policy

No Server or Backend

FoodFetish is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. There is no server, no backend, and no database. All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and IndexedDB.

Google Drive Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, your data is stored in your own Google Drive appDataFolder. The app uses OAuth to authenticate directly with Google. The developer has no access to your Google account or your synced data.

No Data Collection

The developer does not collect, store, or process any personal data. Your data stays on your device and optionally in your own Google Drive.

No Cookies or Tracking

This app does not use cookies for tracking purposes. No analytics services are integrated.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. There are no ads, no analytics providers, and no data brokers involved.
